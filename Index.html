My Therapeutic Approach

Evidence-based techniques combined with compassionate care

Client-Centered Therapy

I believe you are the expert on your own life. My role is to provide a supportive environment where you can explore your feelings, gain insight, and make positive changes.

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

We'll work together to identify and change negative thought patterns and behaviors that may be holding you back from living your best life.

Mindfulness-Based Techniques

Learn practical tools to stay present, manage stress, and respond to life's challenges with greater clarity and calm.
